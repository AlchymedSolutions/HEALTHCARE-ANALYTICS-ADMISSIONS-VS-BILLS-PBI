09
Denial rate by insurance provider
Which insurers deny the most claims?
Slide explanation: Aetna and Medicaid each show a 33% claim denial rate — meaning 1 in 3 claims filed against these payers gets rejected. UnitedHealth denies 25% and Medicare has zero denials in this dataset. Prioritising appeal workflows and pre-authorisation steps for Aetna and Medicaid claims can recover significant revenue.
